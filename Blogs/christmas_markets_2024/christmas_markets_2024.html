Christmas Markets of 2024

Winter 2024

The Motivation

Christmas is a festival that is known by almost everyone in the world. It is celebrated as a fun event by even people of other religious backgrounds. Christmas being an event that occurs in the winter is one of the things people look forward to when the sun shines less and less every day and there is almost no motivation for people to move out of their comfort zones.

Christmas Markets are one of the things that create motivation for people to move out of their houses and walk around with their loved ones. They find reasons to visit other cities, go on dates, or maybe just go out to grab a cup of hot gluwein(kinderpunch in my case LOL)

Once I saw what these Christmas Markets had to offer, especially those beautiful mugs, I decided to go to as many of them as possible and make the most of the time that people had been telling me is one of the most depressing times of the year.

Cologne

Cologne is one of the most active cities for Christmas Markets.

I looked on Google Maps and found that the best way to reach the place would be to catch an auto. Finding the shop's location was not a big deal it was right on the side of the road and had huge boards to welcome people. What surprised me was the change in the ambiance as I walked inside the shop.

I am very well aware that I have not yet mentioned the name of the place I'm visiting, although you might have seen the spoiler in the title. That's because it's an entire chapter in this story.

China Bistro

The change in ambiance is very apparent as you walk into the hallway from a dusty highway filled with vehicles and people. The noises suddenly disappear, and the atmosphere becomes serene. The transition from chaos to order is apparent.
