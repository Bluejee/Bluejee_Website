LIBS for All

Lasers, a marvel of scientific discovery, have proven invaluable in solving innumerable challenges.

Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS), a branch of laser physics, involves directing laser pulses onto materials, studying the resulting plasma to gain insights into the sample.

Introducing OpenLIBS: an open-source Python package designed for the computational analysis of LIBS spectra. It empowers users to conduct qualitative analysis based on LIBS data.

How Intelligent are Ants?

Ants are simple creatures, and often seem to be stupid when looked at individually.

As a colony though, it's a different story. They have amazing mechanisms through which they collaborate and solve problems to figure out how to survive and forage food.

Understanding Complex Behaviours in Multi-Agent systems using Reinforcement Learning, is a project which aims to gain insights on the emergent phenomena that can be observed in the physical world using Reinforcement Learning and Physics

Deciphering Chaos

The allure of chaos captivates us all, evoking notions of freedom and possibility. Terms like the butterfly effect have become common even for teenagers, yet chaos remains a misunderstood enigma.

Contrary to popular portrayal, delving into chaos theory offers insights that underpin our world.

"Chaos - Interpreting the Physical Pendulum and the Chaotic Ratchet", is a project that aims to explore the field of dynamical systems such and study the counterintuitive workings of the physical pendulum and the Chaotic Ratchet.
